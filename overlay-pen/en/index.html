Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-01
This Privacy Policy applies to the Android app "Overlay Pen" (the "App").

1. Overview

The App is a tool that lets users draw and annotate over the screen. The App is designed to minimize the collection of user data wherever possible.

2. Data Not Collected

At this time, the App does not send, collect, or share the following information with the developer's servers.

  • Personal information such as name, address, phone number, or email address
  • Account information
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, audio, or other media files
  • Advertising ID
  • Analytics data
  • Purchase information

3. Data Temporarily Processed On Device

While the App is in use, it temporarily handles drawing-related state in device memory. This may include strokes drawn by the user, the selected color, stroke width, opacity, and current tool state.

  • This data is not transmitted off the device
  • This data is not shared with the developer or third parties
  • This data is cleared when the drawing session ends
  • The App does not currently provide persistent save functionality

4. Permissions and Features Used

The App uses Android overlay-related permission so it can display drawing controls over other apps. The App also uses a foreground service so the overlay drawing feature remains active and stable while in use.

5. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not sell, share, or otherwise provide user data to third parties. At this time, the App also does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, billing SDKs, or crash reporting SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not primarily directed to children under the age of 13.

7. Security

The App reduces privacy risk by avoiding transmission of user data off the device. If future versions add save, sharing, billing, analytics, or similar features that change how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to legal changes, product changes, or operational updates. If material changes are made, notice will be provided through the app, the distribution page, or this public page.
